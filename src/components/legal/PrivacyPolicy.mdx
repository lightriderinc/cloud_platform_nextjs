You have the right to:

- **Limit use of sensitive personal information** — not applicable, as we do not use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the Service

## 9. Public Demo (Pre-Registration)

Before the full platform launches, we offer a free public demo of select features that does not require account registration (the "**Demo**"). This section describes how this Policy applies to the Demo specifically.
<br/>
### What we collect in the Demo

- **No account data** — since the Demo requires no registration, we do not collect names, emails, or passwords through it.
- **Usage & analytics data** — the same analytics tool used on the live platform (see Section 2) also runs on the Demo, collecting pages viewed, features used, session duration, and device/browser information.
- **Technical data** — IP address (used to infer approximate location) and log data, collected automatically.
- **Any content you type into the Demo** — not linked to an identity, but treated as personal information if it could identify you (e.g. if you paste in sample data containing names).
<br/>
### How Demo data is used and retained

- Solely to operate, monitor, and improve the Demo and inform development of the full platform.
- Not sold or shared with third parties, consistent with Section 5.
- Demo content/session data is not guaranteed to be retained and may be cleared at any time; analytics data collected during the Demo is retained on the same basis as described in Section 7.
- If you later register for the full platform, Demo usage data is not linked to your new account unless you were tracked through a persistent identifier (e.g. the same device/cookie) and our analytics tool associates the two sessions.

Your GDPR and CCPA rights described in Section 8 apply equally to any personal information collected through the Demo.
